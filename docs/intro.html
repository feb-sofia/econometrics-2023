The dataset contains the plate numbers and the time capture time from 90 fixed traffic cameras around the small ring of Sofia (Figure 1.1). The cameras captured 647,942 unique plate numbers in a total of 6,155,692 snapshots between 2022-10-01 and 2022-10-31 23:59:59. The timestamp of the snapshot in unavailable for 7 cases (0.0 percent). The cameras failed to recognize the plate number in 549,130 cases (8.9 percent).

1.1 Prep

An exploratory analysis of the data reveals a large number of consecutive snapshots from one and the same camera of the same vehicle in a short period of time. In order to avoid counting these snapshots as multiple inbound or outbound moves, we take the first snapshot with the smallest time stamp (earliest snapshot), removing (XXX, w) subsequent snapshots from the same camera.
